Privacy Policy of SectorEats

1. Introduction

Welcome to SectorEats! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform (Website and Mobile Application) to access services provided by SectorEats.

By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of your data as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our services to you. This includes:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, delivery address, payment information, etc.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our services, including IP address, browser type, device information, and session data.
  • Location Data: We may collect location-based information for delivery purposes, which can be enabled or disabled in your device settings.
  • Payment Data: Payment details may be processed by third-party payment gateways, and we do not store sensitive payment information directly.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To process orders and deliver food or related services to you.
  • To communicate with you, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • To personalize your experience on the platform.
  • To send promotional offers, newsletters, and updates (with your consent).
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

4. Sharing Your Information

SectorEats does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. However, we may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that help us deliver our services, such as payment processors, delivery partners, and marketing agencies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a legal request, such as a subpoena or court order.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

With you, and comply with our legal obligations. Cookies, pixel tags, and other similar technologies are used for various purposes like:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Analyze platform usage and improve performance.
  • Serve personalized ads or promotions.

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

7. Security of Your Data

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

8. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access and receive a copy of your data.
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your data.
  • The right to restrict or object to processing of your data.
  • The right to data portability.

If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@sectoreats.com.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page. Please review this policy periodically for updates.
